📲 Add to Home Screen
To get a tap icon on your home screen (Safari only):
1. Tap the Share ↑ button at the bottom of Safari
2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
3. Edit the name if desired, then tap Add

⚠️ Must be opened from Safari — not Chrome or Firefox.
Once installed, open from your home screen for the full app experience (no browser bar, full screen).
